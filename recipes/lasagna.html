Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 16 oz of lasagna noodles
  • 1 lbs lean ground beef
  • 16 oz of spagehtti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 lbs shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 lbs shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 pt ricotta cheese

Directions

Step 1

  1. Boil large pot of lightly salted water
  2. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente;drain

Step 2

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In a large skillet over medium-high head, brown beef and season with salt pepper; drain
  3. Stir spaghetti sauce and garlic and simmer 5 minutes

Step 3

  1. In a medium bowl, combine cheeses and stir
  2. Alternate layer of noodles, meat mixture, and cheese mixture

Step 4

  1. Bake in oven for 30 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly
