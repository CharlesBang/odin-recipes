Taro Milk Tea

Ingredients

  • 150g taro peeled and cubed
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • salt
  • 2 tbsp tapioca pearls
  • 1/2 cup ceylon tea strongly brewed (or any black tea)
  • 200 ml milk
  • 1 tsp condensed milk

Directions

Step 1

  1. Boil the cubed taro root for 20 minutes on medium heat.
  2. It should be soft enough to poke through with a fork.

Step 2

  1. Discard the water. Smash the cooked ground root until an even paste is formed.
  2. For a smoother paste, use a food processor or blender. Incorporate the sugar while the paste is still hot. Set aside.

Step 3

  1. Add your boba to a pot of boiling water. Add 2 teaspoon of sugar and allow this boil for 5-7 minutes.
  2. Once the boba begin to float these are done. Adjust the timing depending on how firm or soft you prefer them. Remove from hot water and set them aside.

Step 4

  1. Brew the ceylon and let this mix well with fresh ground root paste and condensed milk.

Step 5

  1. Finally, add the milk and tapioca pearls to the mixture. Serve the taro milk tea hot or cold.
